Uri Flush 3 liquid





Uri Flush 3 liquid is to be taken by affected persons 2 tea spoons full (10ml) twice daily. In severe cases 1 table spoon full (15ml) may be taken twice daily. Recommended to take for at least one month and consult a physician or a healthcare professional as required.

Elements Uri Flush-3 Liquid helps dissolve most of the major urinary stone over time with regular usage. It also has a diuretic action that helps a smooth flow of urine and helps flush out stones faster from the urinary tract over time.



Key Ingredients
  • Gokhru (Tribulus terrestris)
  • Punarnava (Boerhaavia diffusa)
  • Pashanabhed (Rotula Aquatica)
  • Shallaki (Boswellia serrata Gumoleoresin)
  • Mountain knot grass (Aerva lanata)
  • Mulethi (Glycyrrhiza glabra)
  • Kulathi (Macrotyloma uniforum)
  • Haridra (Curcuma longa)
  • Shuddha Shilajit (Asphaltum)
  • Guduchi (Tinospora cordifolia)
  • Ajwain (Trachyspermum ammi)

Key Benefits
  • The combination of herbs also helps in keeping the urinary tract free from microbial infection
  • Prevents the formation of stones in the kidneys or ureters in those customers who are susceptible to such stone formation
  • Helps to reduce the size of stones of all types if already formed in the kidneys or ureters
  • Promotes slow but effective reduction in the size of stones of all types and help them to be flushed out through the urine

Directions For Use
  • 1 teaspoon full twice a day before food
  • Recommend drinking of adequate quantities of water and fruit juices additionally

Safety Information
  • Read the label carefully before use
  • Do not exceed the recommended dose
  • Keep out of the reach of children
  • Store in cool and dry place
  • Should be protected from direct light
